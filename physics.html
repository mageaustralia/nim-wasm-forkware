NimParticles  forkware

Thousands of particles, integrated every frame by a real Nim program compiled to WebAssembly in your browser - the kind of per-frame N-body work you can't fake in hand-written JS. Move the mouse to attract, hold the button to repel. Then open Engine (Nim), rewrite the FORCE LAW - Newtonian gravity, a vortex, a spring - press Recompile, and the whole swarm obeys your physics. Save Fork to pass your universe on.
