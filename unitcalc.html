UnitCalc  forkware

A document that carries its own compiler. The calculator below is a real Nim program, compiled to WebAssembly entirely in your browser. Open the Engine (Nim) tab, change a unit factor or add a formula, press Recompile - the tool rebuilds itself live. Then Save Fork to pass your version on as one self-contained file. No server, no install, no account.

Pipeline: Nim 2.2.4 (wasm) → C → clang (wasm) → wasm-ld (wasm) → your engine. Compiler toolchain by benagastov/Nim-WASM-Compiler. The engine is instantiated with a built-in WASI shim and called through its exported evalBuf.
