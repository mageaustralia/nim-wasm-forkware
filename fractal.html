NimFractal  forkware

Every pixel here is millions of iterations of a complex-number formula, run by a real Nim program compiled to WebAssembly in your browser. Click to zoom (shift-click to zoom out). Then open Engine (Nim), rewrite the iteration formula - z = z² + c - into something else, press Recompile, and you're exploring a different fractal entirely. Save Fork to keep your universe and pass it on.
